Importance and facts

Key points

  • Good mental health is important for pre-teen and teenage development, relationships and resilience.
  • Depression, anxiety and behavioral disorders are among the leading causes of illness and disability among adolescents.
  • Physical health is key to mental health. Encourage pre-teens and teenagers to stay active, eat well, sleep, and avoid alcohol and other drugs.
  • The consequences of failing to address adolescent mental health conditions extend to adulthood, impairing both physical and mental health and limiting opportunities to lead fulfilling lives as adults.
